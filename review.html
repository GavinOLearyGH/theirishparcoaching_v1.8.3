How to Enter

Scorecard shorthand

Fairway = Y | L | R | S | O
Hit fairway, or miss left / right / short / long.
Green = Y134 | L125 | S140
(hit distance or miss + distance)
Putts = 1–4
Short Game = U/D Y/N    S/S Y/N
Example hole (Par 4)
R / L125 / 2 / N / N
Right tee shot • Missed green left (125) • 2 putts
